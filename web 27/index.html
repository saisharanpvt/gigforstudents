What is GIG - The Last Minute Saviour?

GIG (The Last Minute Saviour) is a comprehensive digital platform and educational community specifically designed for Telangana Polytechnic Diploma students. It acts as an all-in-one student super-app that provides instant access to essential academic resources and tools to help students prepare for exams effectively.

It provides instant access to study materials, previous year question papers, E-books, Learning Videos, ECET mock tests to help students prepare for exams effectively.
