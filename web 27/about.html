Terms & Policies
Last Updated: March 2026
1. Information Only Platform ▼
GIG is a facilitator that connects students. We do not own the items listed in the rental section, nor do we guarantee the accuracy of academic materials shared by other users. Use the information at your own discretion.
2. Rentals & Transactions ▼
GIG does not handle payments. All financial transactions are strictly peer-to-peer (between students). We are not responsible for:
  • Lost, damaged, or stolen items.
  • Payment disputes or fraud.
  • Condition of the rented/sold items.
Please verify the item and the person before making any payment.
3. Community Guidelines ▼
The "Nexus" chat and public forums are for academic and social growth. Zero Tolerance Policy: Any hate speech, bullying, harassment, or sharing of explicit content will result in an immediate permanent ban without warning.
4. AI & Tools Disclaimer ▼
The "GIG Mate" AI is a simulation designed to assist with studies. It may occasionally provide incorrect information. Always verify critical syllabus details with your official college faculty or textbooks.

The "ID Card" generator is for novelty/backup purposes only and is not a legal government document.
5. Data Privacy ▼
We value your privacy.
  • Your login data is stored locally on your device (LocalStorage) for ease of access.
  • We do not sell your phone number or data to third-party advertisers.
  • Photos uploaded for ID cards are processed in your browser and not stored on our permanent servers.
