About College & Polytechnic
🎓What is Polytechnic?

A 3-year diploma program after Class 10 (SSC/CBSE) offering technical education. It is the fastest route to becoming an engineer.

Why Choose It?
• Practical & Job-Ready Skills.
• Lateral Entry into B.Tech 2nd Year (ECET).
• Affordable & Placement Oriented.

🏫Government Institute of Electronics

Established in 1981, GIOE is a premier technical institute dedicated to electronics and allied engineering fields.

Key Highlights:

📚Curriculum & Exams
  • C-21 / C-24: Updated curriculum aligned with NEP & Industry needs.
  • Structure: 6 Semesters (3 Years).
  • Subjects: Approx 10 per sem (Theory + Labs).
  • Internal Exams: 2 Mids (20 Marks). Pass: 07.
  • Sem End Exam: 40 Marks (Theory). Pass: 14.
📖Syllabus Breakdown
  • Mid 1: Chapters 1 & 2
  • Mid 2: Chapters 3 & 4
  • Final Exam: All Chapters (Focus on 5 & 6)

📋Attendance Rules
  • Required: 75% minimum to appear for exams.
  • Condonation: 65% - 74% (Requires Medical Proof + ₹1300 Fine).
  • Detained: Below 65% (Repeat Year).
  • Biometric: Marked twice daily (Morning & Evening).

🚀Srujana Tech Fest

State-level technical project expo organized by SBTET. The biggest platform for polytechnic innovators.

Levels:
  • College Level: Internal screening.
  • District Level: Inter-college competition.
  • State Level: Grand Finale. Awards & Incubation support.
Registration Process:
  • Form a team (2-5 students).
  • Get a Faculty Guide.
  • Submit Abstract (PDF) & Budget.
  • Present Working Model.
📦Final Year
  • Mandatory Major Project.
  • Industrial Training (6 Months).
  • Campus Placements & Internships.
